1Information We Process and How We Use It

Our App is designed with a privacy-first, on-device architecture. This means we minimize the collection of personal data and prioritize local processing.

A. Camera Data & Real-Time Scanning (On-Device Processing)

  • What we process: the App requires access to your device's camera to detect, capture, and decode QR codes and barcodes.
  • How we handle it: visual data from your camera stream is processed strictly in real-time, in-memory, and locally on your device.
We do not record, save, store, or transmit your camera feed or images to any external servers.

B. Scan History & Local Storage

  • What we process: text contents, barcode formats, custom tags, and timestamps of your scanned items.
  • How we handle it: all scan history is stored strictly on your device using local sandboxed storage (such as browser localStorage or local App container storage). This data is never sent to our servers. You have complete control to search, edit, export (to CSV/JSON), or permanently delete individual items or your entire history at any time.

C. Mobile Subscriptions & Financial Information

  • What we process: premium subscription status and transaction metadata (such as product IDs: qt_barcode_pro_yearly or qt_barcode_pro_monthly).
  • How we handle it: if you upgrade to our Premium tier, all transactions are processed securely and directly by Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card numbers, bank account details, or payment credentials.

D. Local Account & Sandbox Information

  • What we process: local session indicators, language preferences (English, Chinese, Thai), and active state parameters.
  • How we handle it: all settings and local session details are stored locally. The App features an Automatic Inactivity Purge (Sandbox Purge) mechanism. If enabled, this safety feature automatically wipes all local scan logs, settings, and cached session states from your device after a period of inactivity to ensure absolute privacy.

2Device Permissions Required

To deliver core scanning services, the App may request the following system permissions:

  • Camera Permission: required to capture and decode real-time barcodes. You may revoke this permission at any time in your device's operating system settings. If revoked, you can still scan barcode images by uploading them manually.
  • Storage / Photos Access (Optional): required only if you choose to scan a QR code from a pre-existing image file saved on your device, or when exporting your scan history to a local CSV or JSON file.

3Third-Party Services & Advertisements

To support the development and maintenance of our free tier, the App may integrate trusted third-party SDKs. These partners may collect anonymous technical identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: facilitates core system operations and license verification.
  • Google AdMob: serves advertisements within the free version of the App. AdMob may process device advertising identifiers (e.g., Android AD_ID) and anonymous usage telemetry to display relevant ads.
  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): collects anonymous crash logs, device model names, and system versions to help us diagnose bugs and improve App stability.

4Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Data Sales: we do not sell, trade, rent, or monetize your personal information or scanned contents to third parties under any circumstances.
  • No Third-Party Sharing of Scans: the content of your scanned QR codes and barcodes is processed entirely locally and is never shared with third parties, unless you explicitly trigger a native share action to send the scanned text to another app of your choice.

5Security

We implement industry-standard physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to protect your locally stored data. However, because your scan history is stored directly on your own device, the ultimate security of your logs depends on securing your personal device (e.g., utilizing device passcodes or biometric locks).

6Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. If we discover that any anonymous telemetry from a child under 13 has been processed, we will take immediate steps to remove it.
